    What is a Payment Gateway ?

    A Payment Gateway is the “checkout” portal used to enter credit card information or credentials for services.

    How does a payment gateway work?

    Let's take a step further and analyze how a payment gateway works throughout the payment journey.
    Show more + Show less -
    Step 1: First of all, the customers decide to make a purchase, then submit their orders to your site.
    Step 2: The merchant verifies their orders, and sends a response whether it’s a ‘GO’ or it’s a ‘NO’.
    Step 3: After the confirmation arrives, if the customers have enough funds to make the purchase, they have to confirm the payment through a secure payment gateway. Here, the customers need to enter their credit or debit card details on the checkout page.
    Step 4: Next, the payment gateway integrated into your site validates the customer’s card details securely, ensures the funds are available, and performs fraud checks before they send the card data to the acquiring bank.
    Step 5: The acquiring bank securely sends the information to the card schemes, where perform another layer of fraud check, and then send the payment data to the issuing bank.
    Step 6: Step 6: The issuing bank will perform fraud screening and authorizes the transaction. The approved or declined payment message is transferred back from the card schemes, then to the acquirer.
    Step 7: The acquiring bank sends the approval or decline message back to the payment gateway who then transmits the message to the merchant. If the payment is approved, the acquirer collects the payment amount from the issuing bank and eventually enables merchants to get paid.

    How to integrate payment gateway in woocommerce?

    It is really simple to intergrate and configure your information with open-source e-commerce plugin for WordPress - Woocommerce.
    Show more + Show less -
    Step 1: Download

    Please download link: paycec-wordpress-2.0.zip Checksum SHA1: 30AB2D291FF241887596F761CF2D10968D027100

    Note: you need to meet some requirements below

    - A PayCEC account (Merchant’s ID and password)

    - Requires WordPress Version: 4.x

    - Woocommerce requires at least: 2.6

    - Enable CURL in Apache

    Step 2: Install PayCEC Payment Gateway for WooCommerce

    Firstly, Click on Plugins menu on the left sidebar > click Add New button.

    Note: you need to meet some requirements below

    - A PayCEC account (Merchant’s ID and password)

    - Requires WordPress Version: 4.x

    - Woocommerce requires at least: 2.6

    - Enable CURL in Apache

    Next, Click on Upload Plugin button, then click Select file.

    Thirdly, Select file wpc-wordpress-2.0.zip and click Open.

    Subsequently, click on Install Now button.

    After you see status “Plugin installed successfully”, click on Activate Plugin button.

    Step 3: Configure and activate the PayCEC Payment Gateway

    Please visit the flow: WooCommerce > Settings > Payments > PayCEC Gateway

    CASE 1: LIVE MODE IF YOU’RE READY TO GO LIVE

    • 3.1 Go to PayCEC My account at link https://www.paycec.com/myaccount , select your merchant in the main dashboard, click Integration Menu on the left sidebar and disable View test data. You need to copy Merchant Username and Secret Key information to Wordpress dashboard.
    • 3.2 Choose Enable PayCEC GateWay.
      • - Then you need to input Merchant Username and Secret Key.
      • - Untick Test Mode.
      • - You can enter a Title and Description that are displayed to customers in checkout site.
      • - Click on Save changes button to complete the setting.

      - Now, you can acquire payments from customers with PayCEC Payment Gateway on your site.

    CASE 2: TEST MODE

    • 3.3 Go to PayCEC My account at link https://www.paycec.com/myaccount ,select your merchant in the main dashboard, click Integration Menu on the left sidebar and enable Viewing test data. Then copy Merchant Username and Secret Key to Wordpress dashboard.
    • 3.4 TickEnable PayCEC GateWay . Following that paste the copied credentials to the fields of Merchant Username and Secret Key . Tick Test Mode . You can enter a Title and Description that are shown to customers in checkout display. Click on Save changes button.

      - Now, you can acquire payments from customers with PayCEC Payment Gateway on your site.
